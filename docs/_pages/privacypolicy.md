# Privacy Policy

Coffee Journal is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

<br>

## 1.0 Data Collection and Storage

Coffee Journal does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

### 1.1 Local Storage
- All brew logs, equipment data, coffee information, and tasting notes are stored locally on your device
- Data is stored using Core Data with SwiftData on your iOS device

### 1.2 iCloud Sync
- If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced to your personal iCloud account using CloudKit
- This data remains in your personal iCloud storage and is not accessible to us
- You maintain full control over your iCloud data through your Apple ID settings

<br>

## 2.0 What We Do NOT Collect

Coffee Journal does not collect:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Device identifiers
- Any information about your coffee habits or brewing patterns

<br>

## 3.0 Apple Intelligence

Coffee Journal may use on-device Apple Intelligence features to analyze your brew history and suggest parameters:
- All ML processing happens entirely on your device
- No data is sent to external servers
- You can disable these features at any time in Settings

<br>

## 4.0 Third-Party Services

Coffee Journal does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

<br>

## 5.0 Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data:
- There is no data to request, modify, or delete from our servers
- All your data is under your control on your device and in your iCloud
- You can delete all app data by deleting the app from your device

<br>

## 6.0 Children's Privacy

Coffee Journal does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

<br>

## 7.0 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

<br>

## 8.0 Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
