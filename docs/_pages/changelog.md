### `In Development`
# **Version 1.0**
The first release of Coffee Journal - a minimal, monochrome app for tracking your coffee brewing journey.

#### Core Features
- **Equipment Management** - Track grinders, brew methods (V60, Aeropress, espresso, etc.)
- **Coffee Tracking** - Log roasters, origins, roast dates, and varieties
- **Brew Logging** - Record complete brew parameters (grind, dose, yield, temperature, time)
- **Tasting Notes** - 1-5 scale ratings, SCA flavor wheel tags, and custom descriptors
- **Photo Support** - Capture and attach photos to your brews
- **Search & Filter** - Find brews by coffee, method, or date

#### Technical
- **iCloud Sync** - Seamless sync across iPhone and iPad
- **Offline First** - Full functionality without internet connection
- **Privacy Focused** - All data stays in your iCloud, no external servers
- **Apple Intelligence** - On-device ML for brew insights and suggestions
- **Monochrome Design** - E-ink friendly, black and white aesthetic
